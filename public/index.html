Access & Roles

—

Set up two login PINs so front-line staff and the business owner see different things. Staff PIN unlocks Dashboard, New Registration, Active Bay, Invoices & Receipts, Staff, and Expenses. Admin PIN unlocks everything, including Revenue Reports and Settings. Leave both blank for open access with no login screen at all.

Data & Backup

—

Your data lives on the server and is shared live across every device that opens this app — perfect for multiple front-desk terminals. Export a backup regularly and store it somewhere safe.
